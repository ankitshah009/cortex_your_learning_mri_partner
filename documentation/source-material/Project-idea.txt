Product name - Cortex
Primary tagline
See where understanding breaks.
This is stronger than “See how students think” because it is precise, active, and tied directly to the product’s job.
Secondary line:
Cortex traces a learner’s reasoning, tests its diagnosis, and finds the smallest intervention that unlocks progress.
One-sentence value proposition
Cortex is an AI learning diagnostic that identifies the first point where a learner’s reasoning breaks, verifies the underlying misconception through adaptive questioning, and generates the next best learning step.Product thesis
Most learning tools evaluate the final answer. AI tutors often skip directly to explanation.
Cortex does something different: it diagnoses before it teaches.
It observes the learner’s reasoning, converts it into a structured map of claims and dependencies, detects the earliest divergence, forms a misconception hypothesis, and asks a targeted question to verify whether that hypothesis is correct.
The output is not “right” or “wrong.”
The output is:

 Here is where your reasoning changed direction.
 Here is what you likely believe.
 Here is the question that proves it.
 Here is the smallest lesson that fixes it.
